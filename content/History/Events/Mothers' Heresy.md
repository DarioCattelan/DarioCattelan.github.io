Mothers' Heresy was a series of overlapping wars waged by the [[Orlins]]  to free the [[Halflings]]  from slavery and servitude of the other races.

# Edict of Axiomatic Equality
Following  [[Grastus the Free]]'s [[Orlins#The Enlightenment of Axiomatic Equality|Enlightenment of Axiomatic Equality]], the Orlin underwent a civil war which resulted in the Orlin adopting a religious imperative for the personal liberty of all [[axiomatic mortals]]. Failing to persuade the neighbouring [[Dwarves]] to release their [[Halflings|Halfling]] slave population, the Orlin invaded.

# The Freewalk Accord
After several notable battles, the Orlins seized huge swath of territory from their adversaries, freeing thousands of Halfling slaves. They agreed to a peace agreement with the Orlin, whereupon the Orlin would return the majority of the territories seized in return for the ban on slavery of [[axiomatic mortals]].
