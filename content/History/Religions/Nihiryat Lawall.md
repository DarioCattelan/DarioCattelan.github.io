The Nihiryat Lawall (nee-HEER-yaht la-WHALL) is the codified religion of the [[Elves]] from [[Elenwe The Farmer]]. 

# Core Beliefs
1. Repetition brings us closer to divine perfection
2. There exist key moments in the stream of time with great power
3. With age comes wisdom, with wisdom comes divinity
