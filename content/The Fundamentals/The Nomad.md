# Description

## Preferred Form
A tall traveller in rough sewn black clothing of a highwayman. He wears a tricorn hat with a swallow tail feather, black greatcoat with iron buttons, leather riding pants stressed and worn, black leather gloves, and thick iron cloud stepping shoes. His face is obscured by a mask, and he carries a bone flute in his coat.

## Emotional State
Intense, cunning, respectful, vengeful, clever.
