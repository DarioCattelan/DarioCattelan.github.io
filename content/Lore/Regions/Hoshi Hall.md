#Region

Hoshi Hall is the name of the realm of the [[Fey Folk]] ruled by [[Titania The Muse]]. It sits in the heart of every forest.

# Dominant Terrain

## The Backwoods
The Backwoods is a mysterious natural landscape that is connected to Hoshi Hall, but doesn't align with anyplace in [[Kaloshen]]. There are many myths and fables of folk travelling to and from Hoshi Hall and finding themselves trapped in the Backwoods, meeting all manner of stranger people and creatures.
