# Speakers
Elven is the language spoken, written, and read by [[Elves]].

# Script
Elven script is looping, serifed, and symmetrical, and whole words are written without lifting the quill from the page. Elven script refuses to be carved, engraved, or tattooed on plants or animals.

# Phonics

Elven is an elegant language of soft consonants and vowels with an almost musical tonality.
